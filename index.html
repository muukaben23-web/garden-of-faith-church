About Our Church

Our Mission

To glorify God by making disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything Jesus commanded.

Our Vision

We envision a transformed Zambia where hope flourishes through Christ-centered initiatives. Our vision is to build orphanages to care for vulnerable children, establish schools that provide quality education with Christian values, create community centers that help the needy, launch radio stations to spread the gospel nationwide, and develop sustainable programs that empower communities spiritually, socially, and economically.

Our Outreach Goals

  • Build orphanages for vulnerable children
  • Establish Christian schools nationwide
  • Support the needy and vulnerable
  • Launch gospel radio stations
  • Community development programs
  • Nationwide evangelism outreach

What We Believe

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God
  • One God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit
  • Jesus Christ is the Son of God, born of a virgin, died for our sins, and rose again
  • Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ
  • The baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence of speaking in tongues
  • Divine healing, miracles, and the return of Jesus Christ
